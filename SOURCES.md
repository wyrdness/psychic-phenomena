## Note on Sources

This bibliography represents diverse perspectives:
- **Proponent sources**: Researchers arguing for reality of psi phenomena
- **Skeptical sources**: Critical analyses and alternative explanations
- **Neutral sources**: Historical and academic treatments
- **Primary sources**: Original research papers and government documents

Readers are encouraged to examine evidence from multiple perspectives and apply critical thinking to all claims, whether supportive or skeptical of psychic phenomena.
